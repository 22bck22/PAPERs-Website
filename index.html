About

Scholarship begins with a question.

Papers is a year-long research society at Phillips Academy Andover that takes students through every stage of academic research — from formulating questions to submitting to peer-reviewed journals.

Students explore topics across biology, economics, social sciences, and political science, producing novel, publication-ready work through a three-term structure with mentorship and structured peer review.

Getting Involved

Join Papers

Papers operates through a chapter model — students get involved by joining or founding a Papers chapter at their school. Every chapter follows the same research framework and gains access to the Papers journal, peer review network, and shared resources.

To get access, reach out to us directly or apply through your school's chapter.
