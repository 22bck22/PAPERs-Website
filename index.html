About PAPERS

Scholarship begins with a question.

PAPERS is a year-long research society at Phillips Academy Andover that takes students through every stage of academic research — from formulating questions to submitting to peer-reviewed journals.

Students explore topics across biology, economics, social sciences, and political science, producing novel, publication-ready work with mentorship and structured peer review.

"Every great discovery began as a carefully crafted question."

Over three academic terms, students build research fluency — learning to craft questions, review existing literature, select methodologies, analyze data, and present findings to academic and public audiences.
